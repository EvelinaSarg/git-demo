Discover the Magic of Rising Stars Art School!

Does your child have a passion for performing?
At Rising Stars Art School, we provide a fun and nurturing environment
where young actors can explore their creativity and develop their skills.
Our school offers drama, singing and dance classes for children aged 3-15.
Our classes help children to build confidence and social skills
and are a huge amount of fun.

Age groups

Students at Rising Stars Art School are divided into age appropriate groups.

  • Mini Stars(3-7)
  • Junior Stars(8-11)
  • Rising Stars(12-15)

Our program includes:

  • Musical theatre workshops
  • Improvisation and stage combat classes
  • Performance opportunities in school productions

In our school, your child will not only learn the craft of acting, but also gain valuable life skills that will serve them well in any pursuit.
